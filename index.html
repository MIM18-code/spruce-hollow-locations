Scout Protocol

Facility Scouting Checklist

Nine non-negotiable items mapped to script requirements. Verify at every candidate site.

  • Central yard with sightlines — Can you see Admin, Mess Hall, Dormitories, and Isolation Block from one standing position? (Scene 37 layout requirement)
  • Multi-building campus — Minimum 4 distinct structures or wings. Single-building facilities cannot convey the "compound" geography the script demands.
  • Long institutional corridors — Dormitory and Admin hallways must support tracking shots. Minimum ~80 feet uninterrupted.
  • Cell or secure room infrastructure — Metal doors, barred windows, small concrete rooms. If absent, confirm space for a practical Isolation Block build.
  • Surrounding dense woods — Escape sequence (Scene 145, 174 blocks) requires adjacent forest accessible for night shoots. No urban backdrop visible.
  • Fire road approach — Unpaved road through tree canopy leading to facility. Must accommodate picture vehicles and crew transport.
  • Structural safety — Asbestos assessment, roof integrity, floor load capacity. Crew safety for 14–18 day occupation. Abandoned ≠ safe.
  • Power and water access — Generator hookup points or existing infrastructure. Bathroom facilities within reasonable distance for crew of 30+.
  • Legal access pathway — Confirmed owner/manager willing to negotiate production agreement. No trespassing-grade sites without formal permission.
